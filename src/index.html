How to play?

1. To start the game you will receive $200 that you can spin.

2. You always have to mark the value you want to play with: ready-made (5$, 10$, etc.) or enter it manually in the space provided and mark the coin.

3. You cannot bet more value than you have in your wallet.

4. You cannot bet at the same time with a predefined deposit amount (5$, 10$, etc.) and bet by manual entry.

5. You cannot enter a zero or negative number in a manually entered value.

6. If you would like to deselect a value marked in a deposit you can unmark it with a double click - same for coins.

7. Your wallet value will decide which level you reach and which rewards you can claim.

8. Remember that when you refresh the page, your entire result will reset to the original state.
